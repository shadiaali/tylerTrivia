Anatomy of the 'Add Questions' form


Box 1: Choosing the type of round question

You must choose the correct round question type to bring up the proper form. Each round corresponds to a different type of question.

  • Round One: Mixed Bag Questions
    1 open ended question + 1 answer

  • Round Two: Have you Seen the Scene? Questions
    1 question/hint + 5 answer/choice options + 1 correct answer + 1 mp4 video clip

  • Round Three: Match Game Questions
    1 question/hint/instruction + 5 questions/options + 5 correct answers/choices/matches + 7 incorrect answers/choices/matches

  • Round Four: Name that Tune Questions
    1 question/hint + 1 mp3 audio + 1 answer

  • Category Images for Jeopardy
    an image for each jeopardy category

  • Round Five: Jeopardy Questions (categories 1-5)
    [Question type 1: open ended question + answer]
    or
    [question type 2: Question/Hint + Song clip + Answer]
    or
    [question type 3: Question/Hint + Image/Gif + Answer]

  • Final Round: Questions
    [Type 1 - Open Ended Question + Answer]
    or
    [ Type 2 - Multiple Options + Video + Answer]
    or
    [Type 3 - Audio + Question/hint + answer ]


Box 2: Insert into Round

Based on the type of round question you chose in the first select box, the second box will auto-fill with the matching round your question will be inserted into.

This option is what will separate your questions into the proper rounds. Any type of question can be inserted into any round; it is not limited to the option that is auto-chosen. For instance, you could choose option 1: Round 1: Mixed Bag for a question and then insert that question into a round that was not Round One by choosing a different option in this box (box 2).


Box 3: Question Title.


Give your question a title. This will show on the main questions list and will be seen by the users/viewers. It can be anything you want - e.g, 'Question 1' or '1: Keanu Reeves'

Round One: Mixed Bag Questions


Open ended question + answer

This question type is for an open ended question and answer.

Enter your question and answer, and hit submit.

Your question will appear on the 'Questions' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Your answer will appear on the 'Answers' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Round Two: Have you seen the scene? Questions


1 question/hint + 5 answer/choice options + 1 correct answer + 1 mp4 video clip

This question type is for a question or hint, five answers/choice options, one correct answer, and one video clip.

Enter your question or hint, your 5 answers/choices, 1 correct answer, the name of your mp4 clip (should have been uploaded to the images folder via FTP) and then hit submit.

Your question will appear on the 'Questions' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Your answer will appear on the 'Answers' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Round Three: Match Game Questions


1 question/hint/instruction + 5 questions/options + 5 correct answers/choices/matches + 7 incorrect answers/choices/matches

This question type is for a question or hint, five questions/options, five correct answers/matches, and seven incorrect answers/choices/matches.

Enter your question or hint, your 5 questions/options, 5 correct answers/matches, 7 incorrect answers/choices/matches and then hit submit.

Your question will appear on the 'Questions' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Your answer will appear on the 'Answers' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Round Four: Name that Tune Questions


1 question/hint + 1 mp3 audio + 1 answer

This question type is for a question or hint, mp3 audio file, and 1 answer.

Enter your question or hint, upload your .mp3 audio file (around 30 sec is a good length), add your answer, and hit submit.

Your question will appear on the 'Questions' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Your answer will appear on the 'Answers' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Category Images for Jeopardy


5 Images or gifs - one for each category of Jeopardy

This question type is for batch uploading 5 category images or gifs for Jeopardy.

Each upload box corresponds to a Jeopardy category. You can upload either images (jpg, jpeg,png) or gifs (gif). Do not upload more than one batch of images. You should delete your first batch of category images before uploading a new one.

Your category images will appear on the Jeopardy category page.

Round Five: Jeopardy Questions (categories 1-5)


[Question type 1: open ended question + answer] or [question type 2: Question/Hint + Song clip + Answer] or [question type 3: Question/Hint + Image/Gif + Answer]

You must first choose a Category of Jeopardy to insert your question into. The corresponding round will be auto-chosen for you.

For the Jeopardy type question, you are able to choose from 3 different question types.

[Question type 1: open ended question + answer]

Your question will appear on the 'Questions' page, under the Jeopardy Category round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Your answer will appear on the 'Answers' page, under the Jeopardy category round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

[question type 2: Question/Hint + Song clip + Answer]

Your question will appear on the 'Questions' page, under the Jeopardy Category round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Your answer will appear on the 'Answers' page, under the Jeopardy category round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

[question type 3: Question/Hint + Image/Gif + Answer]

Your question will appear on the 'Questions' page, under the Jeopardy Category round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Your answer will appear on the 'Answers' page, under the Jeopardy category round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Final Round (question types 1-3)


[Type 1 - Open Ended Question + Answer] or [ Type 2 - Multiple Options + Video + Answer] or [Type 3 - Audio + Question/hint + answer ]

For the Final round, you are able to choose from 3 different types of questions.

[Type 1 - Open Ended Question + Answer]

Your question will appear on the 'Questions' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

Your answer will appear on the 'Answers' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

[ Type 2 - Multiple Options + Video + Answer]

Your answer will appear on the 'Answers' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.

[Type 3 - Audio + Question/hint + answer ]

Your question will appear on the 'Questions' page, under the round you inserted it into, with the title you gave it.
